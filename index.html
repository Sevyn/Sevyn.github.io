My work experiences and skills

Brief overview of co-op positions held

My name is Joanna Weng and I am a 3rd year Honours Arts and Business student at the University of Waterloo.

My personal brand is that I aim to assist developers and designers in achieving their goals to present the full potential of their products to the public as a Project Manager of a large technological company. I want to help creative individuals build and promote their own brands by utilizing project management, marketing strategies, and consulting tactics that I will gain throughout my academic and coop terms. The industry that I primarily want to focus my specialization in is software development and technology because I am fascinated by possible technological advances.

As a project coordinator for D2L Inc., I was given full responsibility of various projects from managers and senior executives. Some of the larger projects included creating an Outage Matrix, data mining reports, and coordinating software monitoring, migrations, performances, SQL scripts, and capacity planning initiatives. I also created project reports, workflows, spreadsheets and documentation for executive review in order to target issues and develop improvements. Due to the holistic software technology environment, I was able to hone my time management and leadership skills through managing simultaneous projects, communicating with various departments, and adhering to strict deadlines.

I held a Customer Service position at Lug Canada, but grew to take on Business Development responsibilities. I learned to utilize Netsuite to manage the company sales by organizing orders, generating reports, and managing customer profiles. I worked with end consumers with their purchases, warranty claims, returns/exchanges, and inquiries which greatly improved my interpersonal and problem solving skills. I also coordinated with various vendors on a daily basis to handle their mass orders, ASNs, EDIs, and inventory reports.

My upcoming Spring coop term will be as an IT Project Coordinator at CIBC.

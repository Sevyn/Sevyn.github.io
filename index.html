About Me

My name is Joanna Weng and I have over 7 years of experience in product management and over 10 years of experience in the technology industry. I have a passion for designing as a hobby which blends well with my work in product management as I am able to bring user experience/design perspective to the table. I am a graduate from the University of Waterloo who studied Legal Studies, Economics, and Cognitive Science with the Honours Arts and Business program. Through my academic courses, I was able to hone my analytical skills and become more knowledgeable about legal proceedings, all aspects of business development, and machine learning. My significant achievements include analyzing statistics and utilizing IBM'S SPSS to recode and interpret the relevant data to develop a consensus report. Additionally, I participated in several business simulations where my teams acted in the role of a Board of Directors by assessing competitors and industry trends to develop strategies to improve the performance of the company. To read samples of my academic work, please refer to my projects.

I have held various positions at industry-leading companies within banking, insurance, consulting, software, and e-commerce. I decided to work at a technology start up in Product Management to experience the fast-pace environment of building a new product. I enjoy working with various teams to design and develop a product and follow it through the Product Lifecycle to ensure a high-quality deliverable. I was able to improve my time management and communication skills through managing simultaneous projects, communicating with various stakeholders, and adhering to strict deadlines. Please refer to experiences to learn more.

Experience

EQ Bank - Senior Product Manager (Digital Banking)

As the Product Lead for the business banking product at EQ Bank, I am responsible for stakeholder management and market research to develop the product strategy, roadmap, and feature definition for the MVP business banking product with digital onboarding, account management, and money movement. I lead a 40-person cross-functional team to develop the banking application to target the small business segment of Canada (~1M).

Thomson Reuters - Product Manager (Digital Commerce)

As a Product Manager at Thomson Reuters, I work within the Digital Commerce team to deliver excellent user-centric trial experiences for B2C SaaS products to ultimately increase conversion rates for subscriptions. I lead all analytic efforts for 4 squads to analyze KPIs, monitor user engagement, and identify feature enhancements which has led to over 10 initiatives added to the product roadmap. I also effectively collaborate with key stakeholders to prioritize product features through presenting data metrics and market research.

Interac Corp. - Product Analyst/Product Manager (Digital Payments)

I was employed at Interac Corp., the Canadian debit card system, focused on digital payments. One of my key responsibilities was the growth of the eCommerce portfolio which included Apple Pay, Google Pay, and a new payment solution. I facilitated the integration of merchants and banks onto the service which led to double-digit transactional growth rates month by month. I led project initiatives to introduce new product features from ideation to market launch. Along with managing the existing eCommerce portfolio, I assisted in the entire product lifecycle of emerging payment solutions from defining user stories to conducting UX testing.

DealTap Group Inc. - Product Analyst/Project Manager

As a Product Analyst at DealTap, I wrote user stories with wireframes uin order to define product features for developers to code and designers to create high-fidelity prototypes. In addition, I stepped up to the role of Project Manager where I manage two teams of 15 software developers through monitoring project velocity, scope, and Agile sprint planning. Through continuous communication with key stakeholders, users, and development teams, I help to ensure that product requirements are accurately documented and developed efficiently in accordance to project timelines. I also create analytical reports for KPIs and metrics using Looker to measure market performance and identify areas for improvement. In my roles, I follow the product throughout the Product Lifecycle to develop features for the highest quality deliverable while simultaneously ensuring that the project is adhering to deadlines.

Ernst & Young - Staff Consultant (Advisory Services)

I was a Staff Consultant for Ernst & Young within Risk Advisory. I assessed and tested clients’ financial, operational and IT internal controls to ensure efficiency and compliance through identifying deviations and risks. I recommended areas in which clients can mitigate risks in order to improve the performance and security of the business. In my time at EY, I have reinforced my knowledge in leading technologies such as SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, and Salesforce. Through my role, I have honed my problem solving, analytical, and documentation skills.

Manulife/John Hancock - Business Intelligence Developer (Co-op)

As a Business Intelligence Developer at Manulife/John Hancock, I researched and developed data visualizations using MicroStrategy to build reports and dashboards for the optimization of workflows and data analysis. I assisted in various projects by analyzing and defining user requirements to identify issues and recommend solutions.

Deloitte Consulting - Business Technology Analyst (Co-op)

I have worked as a Business Technology Analyst with Deloitte Consulting where I provided technological solutions to help clients maximize productivity. I helped support the development of a cloud implementation utilizing Guidewire to drive discoverability and sales. My role consisted of two main responsibilities. The first responsibility was to manage project scope across sprints to facilitate changes in regulatory compliance and other non functional requirements. Additionally, I participated in release testing to discover and categorize technological defects in JIRA to improve the quality and robustness of the software.

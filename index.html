About Me

My name is Joanna Weng and I am a graduate from the University of Waterloo who studied Legal Studies, Economics, and Cognitive Science with the Honours Arts and Business program. Through my academic courses, I was able to hone my analytical skills and become more knowledgeable about legal proceedings, all aspects of business development, and machine learning. My significant achievements include analyzing statistics and utilizing IBM'S SPSS to recode and interpret the relevant data to develop a consensus report. Additionally, I participated in several business simulations where my teams acted in the role of a Board of Directors by assessing competitors and industry trends to develop strategies to improve the performance of the company. To read samples of my academic work, please refer to my projects.

I have held various positions at industry-leading companies within banking, insurance, consulting, software, and e-commerce. I then made the jump from large corporations to work at a technology start up in Product Management. I enjoy working with various teams to design and develop a product and follow it through the Product Lifecycle to ensure a high deliverable. I was able to improve my time management and communication skills through managing simultaneous projects, communicating with various stakeholders, and adhering to strict deadlines. Please refer to experiences to learn more.

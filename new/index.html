About Me

My name is Joanna Weng and I have studied Legal Studies, Economics, and Cognitive Science with the Honours Arts and Business Co-op program at the University of Waterloo. Through my academic courses, I was able to hone my analytical skills and become more knowledgeable about legal proceedings, micro and macro economics, and machine learning. I also had the opportunity to complete co-op terms at various companies where I was able to hone my time management and leadership skills through managing simultaneous projects, communicating with various departments, and adhering to strict deadlines. I especially enjoyed learning to utilize analytical tools to improve company initiatives throughout the SDLC and have a strong desire to further expand my knowledge.
